Questions

Frequently asked.

What types of financial institutions can use Kapix?
Kapix is designed for banks, fintechs, insurance companies, investment firms, and any organisation that makes high-volume financial decisions. Our platform scales from startup to enterprise with the same performance guarantees.
How quickly can Kapix be integrated?
Most integrations go live within 2–4 weeks. Our API-first architecture and pre-built SDKs in Python, Node, Go, and Java mean your engineering team can connect in hours. The remaining time is spent on model calibration and business rule configuration.
Is Kapix compliant with financial regulations?
Yes. Kapix is built with regulatory compliance at its core. We support GDPR, PCI DSS, and local data residency requirements. Every AI decision includes a full explainability report for audit purposes.
What makes Kapix different from legacy risk platforms?
Legacy platforms rely on static rules and batch processing. Kapix uses adaptive AI that learns continuously from live data — delivering decisions 47× faster with higher accuracy. Our models evaluate 186 dimensions per transaction versus the industry average of 12–15.
