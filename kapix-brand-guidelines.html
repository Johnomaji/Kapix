02 — Logo Construction & Usage

Clear Space & Minimum Sizes

Maintain proper clear space around the logo to preserve its visual integrity. The minimum clear space is defined by the height of the central node ("x" unit).

Color Usage Rules

Emerald is reserved for primary CTAs, the logo mark core node, active/hover states, and key data highlights. Never use as a background fill for large areas.
Navy / Graphite are the primary background colours for all dark-mode interfaces. Navy is the base; Graphite is for elevated surfaces.
Signal Blue is for secondary interactive elements, data chart accents, and links on dark backgrounds.
Text hierarchy: White (#FFF) for primary text on dark, Silver for secondary, Mist for tertiary, Ghost for disabled/placeholder.

06 — Tone & Voice

Brand Voice

Kapix speaks with quiet confidence. The voice is clear, direct, and expert — never hype-driven. Every word earns its place. We communicate the way the best engineers and financial minds think: precisely.

Voice Attributes

Precise
No vague claims
Authoritative
Expert, not arrogant
Direct
Clear, no filler
Forward
Future-facing lens
Do This
  • "12ms average decision latency across all transaction types."
  • "Real-time credit assessment powered by multi-dimensional data fusion."
  • "Our models process 84K transactions per second."
  • "Enterprise-grade fraud detection with explainable outputs."
Avoid This
  • "Revolutionary AI that will change everything!"
  • "We're disrupting the entire financial industry."
  • "Our cutting-edge, next-gen platform leverages synergies."
  • "The most amazing, game-changing solution ever."
